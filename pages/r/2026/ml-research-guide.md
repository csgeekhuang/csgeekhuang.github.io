## Key Takeaways

A must-read for anyone doing AI research. John Schulman shares practical advice on how to choose research problems, develop taste, and maintain productive research habits.

### Choosing Problems
- Work on problems that are important and where you can make progress
- Develop good taste by reading broadly and deeply

### Research Habits
- Maintain a research notebook
- Read papers actively, not passively
- Seek out the simplest possible experiments

### Building Expertise
- Go deep on a topic before going broad
- Implement things yourself to build intuition
- Don't be afraid to work on "unfashionable" problems
